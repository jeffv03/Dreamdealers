






	                      By Car/Bus:                      
	Follow the highroad A9, just before entering in
	Montpellier (or after if you come from Spain), take the
	exit Montpellier North... Then you have to seek for
	the Airport/Parc des Expositions direction.


                                                       
	                       By Train:                       
	Travel til the Montpellier station. Then climb up the
	stairs and go to the bus station (just beside). Ask
	for a bus for the Parc des Expositions...


                                                       
	                       By Plane:                       
	Travel til the Montpellier airport. Then take a taxi
	or a bus for the Parc des Expositions...
